Welcome

Many people are not aware of the fact there was already more than 2000 nuclear explosions on planet Earth (please see this list provided by SIPRI). These explosions were conducted as nuclear tests by different countries in rally for more and more advanced nuclear weapons.

This site provides an interactive time-lapse of all nuclear explosions on the Earth since 1945 until the most recent test conducted by DPRK in February 2013. It was created in the frame of The World's First Global Virtual Hackathon. Its purpose is increase awareness of general public about nuclera testing, its consequences and the international activities focudes on global nuclear-test ban represented by CTBTO.

This project uses data from Catalogue of nuclear tests compiled by James E. Lawson. Data for few tests after 1996 were gathered by us from public sources.
